Our Vision

Maktabati is a project of an online library website , it contains Several categories of books , Audio books and supports a book reader (PDF). It is designed to alternate real-world libraries for times of special circumstances as the latest crisis of Coronavirus pandemic and global quarantine. We aspire to reach every reader and provide the best service to our community.
